Our Story

The company started its operation in 2021 in the center of Baku city. The name of the restaurant MeatZade has unique meaning, and consist of combination of two words. In general, restaurant serve Meat, and Zade is one the endings of Azerbaijan surnames. Our mission is to serve the best meat dishes for people in Azerbaijan. Our vision is become one of the leading restaurants in Azerbaijan.
